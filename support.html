Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pair my Clip and Hub with the app?

Open the shARC Bite app and go to the Scan screen. Make sure your Clip and Hub are powered on and within Bluetooth range. The app will detect each device automatically — tap to connect.

How do I calibrate the bite sensor?

Press and hold the button on the Clip for 5 seconds to enter calibration mode, then bite down on the mouthpiece as you would during normal welding use. The app will guide you through the calibration steps on the Dashboard screen.

How do I update my firmware?

Open the OTA screen in the app while connected to your Clip or Hub. If a new firmware version is available, you'll see an update prompt — follow the on-screen steps to complete the update. Keep the device powered and in range during the update.

My device won't connect — what should I check?

Confirm Bluetooth is enabled on your phone, that the Clip/Hub has sufficient battery charge, and that you're within a few feet of the device. If it still won't connect, try restarting Bluetooth on your phone or power-cycling the Clip/Hub (single button press to wake from sleep).

How do I check my battery level?

Battery percentage for both the Clip and Hub is displayed on the Dashboard screen once connected.

I have a hardware issue or defect — what do I do?

Email us at support@sharcbite.com with a description of the issue and your order details, and we'll help troubleshoot or arrange a replacement.

Still Need Help?

If your question isn't covered above, email support@sharcbite.com with as much detail as you can — what device you're using, what you were trying to do, and what happened instead.

Privacy Policy — shARC Bite

Last updated: June 23, 2026

shARC Welding ("we," "us," or "our") operates the shARC Bite mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

The shARC Bite App is designed to communicate directly and exclusively with shARC Bite hardware (the "Clip" and "Hub" devices) over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). The App does not require account creation, login, or any personal identification to function.

The App accesses or processes the following information, all of which stays on your device and the paired shARC hardware:

  • Device identifiers: Bluetooth UUIDs and serial numbers of your Clip and Hub devices, used solely to identify and connect to your hardware.
  • Device status data: Battery level, calibration values, dead zone settings, and bite-sensitivity configuration, used to operate and display the status of your hardware.
  • Firmware version information: Used to check for and apply over-the-air (OTA) firmware updates to your Clip and Hub devices.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contact list, or any other personal information. The App does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking software development kits (SDKs).

How Information Is Used

All information processed by the App is used exclusively to:

  • Identify and connect to your paired shARC hardware
  • Display device status (battery, connection state, calibration) within the App
  • Deliver firmware updates to your hardware via Bluetooth

No data is transmitted to our servers, sold, or shared with third parties. Firmware update files are downloaded from our public release repository; this download does not transmit any information about you or your device back to us.

Data Storage

Device pairing information, calibration settings, and preferences are stored locally on your phone and/or on your shARC hardware. We do not operate a cloud backend that stores your data.

Bluetooth Permissions

The App requests Bluetooth permissions solely to discover, pair with, and communicate with your shARC Clip and Hub devices. The App does not use Bluetooth to track your location or scan for unrelated devices.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by welding professionals and hobbyists and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
